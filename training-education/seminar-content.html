    • News briefs presentation

      • Group editorial discussion, considering:

      • does the story fit under the masthead (fulfil content strategy & deliver value proposition)?
      • does the hook have news value?
      • how well does the headline work?
      • do the headline and standfirst complement each other?
      • does the standfirst sell the story and effectively highlight its most interesting points?
      • does the lead capture the essence of the story and address who, what, when, where, why and how?
      • will the angle produce a compelling read?
      • will answering the key questions give the reader what they need to understand the story?
      • is the required research correctly scoped?
      • will the sources for research supply what is needed?
      • will the sources for quotes supply what is needed?
      • will the required collateral support the story effectively?
      • is there a compelling lead image to complement the headline and standfirst?
      • is anything missing?
      • might the story be better suited to a feature format?
      • what might such a feature need in addition to the above?

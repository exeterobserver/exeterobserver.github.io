University invites views on future of Lower Hoopern Valley

Monday 15 May 2023

Martin Redfern

The University of Exeter is inviting local residents to express their views on the future of Lower Hoopern Valley.

Potential uses for the 38 acre mix of pasture and woodland through which Taddiford Brooke passes are a carbon sequestration and storage facility and a research resource which may involve the construction of buildings on the site.

The consultation runs to 26 May.
